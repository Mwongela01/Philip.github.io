Excel Full Project

In this project, I worked with data on bike purchases to explore the factors that influence a customer buying a bike. I began by cleaning the dataset to remove inconsistencies and ensure accuracy. I used Pivot Tables to analyze the data, identifying patterns and relationships between variables such as customer demographics, income and commute distance. Finally, I created an interactive dashboard to visually present the findings, making it easy to see how different factors impacted bike purchases.

MYSQL Exploratory data analysis

In this project, I performed exploratory data analysis using MySQL to examine how companies were impacted during the COVID-19 period. I queried and analyzed datasets containing information on layoffs, industry performance and company size to uncover patterns and trends. This analysis provided insights into which sectors were most affected and how workforce reductions varied across industries, helping to paint a clear picture of the pandemic impact on the corporate landscape.

Power BI Full Project

In this project, I analyzed a Data Professionals Survey using Power BI to uncover valuable insights about the industry. I began by cleaning and preparing the dataset in Power Query to ensure accuracy and consistency. The data included information on job roles, skills, salaries and career satisfaction. I then transformed it into interactive, visually engaging dashboards that highlight key trends, such as in-demand skills, salary distributions and career growth patterns. The final visualizations make it easy for viewers to explore the data and quickly grasp the factors shaping the careers of data professionals.
